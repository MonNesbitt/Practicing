    Hello! I am a PhD candidate in Linguistics at Michigan State University. I am interested in sociolinguistics, phonetics, phonology and their interfaces. In particular, I am interested in probing phonological representations using perceptual and acoustic methodologies. I am also interested in the relationships between social identity and phonological representations.
